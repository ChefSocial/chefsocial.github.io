Menu - 1
Appetizer
Murg Tawa Fry(Chicken)

Tender chunks of chicken marinated in a thick mixture of homemade instant spice mix and roasted over low and high heat and then tawa fried to bring in all flavours together.Served with green creamy chutney.

Main Course
Nawabi Biryani(Chicken)

Commonly known as Hyderabadi Biryani, it's a rare combination of raw(kachcha) gosht with aromatic spices and herbs gently mixed together with flavoured basamati rice in a layered pattern with fried onions and corriander leaves.

Dessert
Shahi Jaan Tukdra

Creamy milk boiled with saffron and sugar to bring it to right texture and used to top the baked bread and garnished with loads of dry fuits and served chill.

Menu - 2
Appetizer
Shikampur Kebab

Chef's special dish similar to shammi kebabs made in 2 versions using minced meat. The dish delivers its name from the word "SHIKAMPUR".

Main Course
Darbaari Biryani(Veg)

Basamati rice and vegetables cooked to perfection with homemade instant spice mix in a dumpkt style. Most flavourful biryani.

Dessert
Shahi Sheer Khurma

A creamy sweet delight served chill after the main course which cools you completely.A dessert made from roasted vermicelli and lots of dry fruits cooked to perfection and garnished with pods of saffron. Served chill at your table.

Menu - 3
Appetizer
Murg Kebabs(Chicken)

Chunks of chicken uniformly cut and marinated in yogurt and homemade instant spice mix and kept overnight and deep fried and served.

Main Course
Pulau + Chicken Khurma + Raitha

Light aromatic spices fried in oil and cooked in rice and served with chicken khurma and raitha.

Dessert
Pasthuni Muzzafar

Par cooked rice flavoured with lots of rosewater and saffron to bring it to perfection. Cream milk flavoured with cardamom powder and dry fruits used as a garnish.

1. When the event started

Menu for the event

The "Mughlai Event" started with Chef socialising with our guests, introducing himself and laying out the menu for them which included delectable dishes like Shikampur Kebabs, Mutton Dum Fusion Biryani and Shahi Sheer Kurma.

Chef Tabrez

Our Chef for the evening, Chef Tabrez, specializes in Mughalai cuisine and adds a unique flavour to all his dishes by his self-prepared "secret" spices. He also experiments and creates a mouth-watering blend of Indian and Mughlai cuisine. The Mutton Dum Fusion Biryani that he prepared at the event was a testimony to this.

Chef Tabrex explaining the guests about the ingredients used

The event had guests/food enthusiasts from different background- consultants, businessmen, founders, CEO's, student, developers, community managers etc. The event was held at the startup house of Travel Life Exploring with an agenda to engage the guests actively in the preparation of the dinner along with our Chef Tabrez.
