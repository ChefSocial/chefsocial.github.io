  • Event at Explore Life Travelling

1. When the event started

Menu for the event

The "Mughlai Event" started with Chef socialising with the guests, introducing himslef and explaining about the menu. The menu included Shikampur Kebabs, Mutton Dum Fusion Biryani and Shahi Sheer Kurma.

Chef Tabrez

The Chef for the event was Chef Tabrez - He specializes in Mughalai cuisine and brings the original flavour to all his dishes by self prepared "secret" masalas. He also experiments with fusion of Indian and Mughlai cuisine to bring that unique falvour to one's taste buds. One similar example was seen at the event too - Mutton Dum Fusion Biryani.

Chef Tabrex explaining the guests about the ingredients used

The event had guests/food enthusiasts coming from different backgrounds - consultants, businessmen, founders, CEO's, student, developers, community managers etc. The event was held at the startup house of Travel Life Exploring with an agenda to engage the guests in the preparation of the dinner along with the Chef Tabrez. This gave them an opportunity to learn more about Mughlai cuisine, how it's done, the ingredients used and various other skills to take home.

We aim at engaging our customers with chefs to prepare food. That's when they learn and take home a delicious experience.

— Rachit Garg

2. When there were curious guests

Chef Tabrez having a discussion with our curious guest - Tom

The event progressed further with Chef explaining more about the Mughlai cuisine as he started his preparations for the dishes. He explained in detail about the ingredients used and their origin.

Chef explaining about the knives
Chef demostrating a cutting technique to our guest - Nisha
Chef explaining about the ingredients and their origin
Chef demostrating the roll making technique for Shikampur Kebabs

The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing.

He also demonstrated various cutting techniques and other skills to make shikampur kebab's rolls. He also explained the usage of various knives that are used by professional chefs.

Chef serving and explaining about Shikampur Kebabs to everyone
Chef explaining the recipe of Shahi Sheer Kurma to our guest - Vikram

Chef left an open ground for discussions where the guests were curious to know about the mughali cuisine, ingredients, recipes, his secret masalas, various cooking skills and techniques.

3. When our guests had fun

Our guest - Abhik and Arthur(left to right) having fun while preparing

People want to be creatively satisfied, and having fun is such an important part of that.

— John Lasseter

Apart from getting to know more about the science involved in cooking and various skills and techniques,the event also helped the guests to know each other. It was a social experience where curious food enthusiasts from different backgrounds came together at one place to learn about cooking and take home a socialising experience of cooking with a professional chef which they couldn't have found at any star rated hotels.

4. When the food was ready

Shahi Sheer Kurma served in authentic earthen pots
Shikampur Kebabs draped with silver foil
Chef Tabrez serving the delicious Mutton Dum Fusion Biryani

We are judged by what we finish, not by what we start.

The authentic mughlai dishes came out really delicious and gave the exact flavour which one would find in Mughlai cuisine. Serving them in their traditional manner(Shahi Sheer Kurma in earthen pots and Shikampur Kebabs draped with silver foil) also added to the authenticity of the dishes.

They also appreciated the concept of 'food socialising' and happily took home various skills and techniques along with a social experience to cook/prepare along with different people and a professional chef.
